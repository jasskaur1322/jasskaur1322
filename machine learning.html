Advantages

Advantages of Machine Learning

Fast & Accurate Predictions

Processes vast amounts of data rapidly with high precision and accuracy.

Automates Repetitive Tasks

Improves Decision-Making

Slide 07

Disadvantages

Disadvantages of Machine Learning

Requires Large Data

Needs massive, quality datasets to train models effectively.

High Development Cost

Infrastructure, talent, and computing resources are expensive.

Risk of Biased Results

Models can inherit and amplify biases from training data.

Privacy Concerns

Handling sensitive personal data raises serious privacy issues.
