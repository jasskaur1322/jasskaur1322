Namaste! 🙏 I'm Jaspreet Kaur, an 18-year-old creative soul from Bharthgarh, Punjab. I believe in the power of handmade craftsmanship and the beauty of turning raw materials into something meaningful.

I specialize in partition making — crafting intricate room dividers and decorative panels — and dress designing — creating beautiful outfits that blend tradition with modern style.

After completing my 12th standard in 2025, I've now embarked on a new journey at ITI Women Ropar, where I'm sharpening my technical skills to build a brighter future.
